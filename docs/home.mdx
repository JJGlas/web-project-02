# Home

Welcome to a collection of comprehensive guides based on personal experience, research and observations. 

The guides on the side bar cover:
- **Video Encoding, Audio, and Muxing** through use of my preferred CLI and GUI tools. For example, you may want to create a cutting edge efficiently compressed AV1 video with E-AC-3 5.1 channel surround audio, and tidy metadata that states: Track languages, encoding CLI command/parameters used, and a video title. Naturally you will encode video and audio at the same time, as they are in a container together. So we will look at **audio formats**, how to edit **metadata**, preserve **HDR** and **mux** or **remux**. This guide is intended to inform you enough to get you started and give you additional tips; it is not an entire wiki of every CLI parameter and aspect.

All the tools and methods discussed are ones I personally use and highly recommend.
